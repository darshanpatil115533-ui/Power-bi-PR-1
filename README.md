## 📖 Project Overview

The **Super Stock Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, quantity sold, and discounts. It provides valuable business insights through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

✅ Monitor overall sales performance  
✅ Analyze profit across different regions  
✅ Track monthly sales trends  
✅ Compare product category performance  
✅ Support data-driven business decisions  

---

## 📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

📈 **Total Sales**  
💰 **Total Profit**  
📦 **Total Quantity Sold**  
🏷️ **Total Discount**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 📊 Sales by Category
- Compare sales across product categories.
- Identify top-performing categories.

### 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
- Analyze sales growth over time.
- Detect seasonal patterns and trends.

### 🍩 Profit by Region
- Evaluate profitability across regions.
- Identify strong and weak markets.
